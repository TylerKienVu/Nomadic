Description

Irvine Spectrum is an outdoor shopping center that is comprised of over 130 stores, restaurants, and entertainment venues. There are a range of stores from Target to Barnes and Noble Booksellers to Vans. When taking a break from shopping, one can choose from a variety of restaurants from casual fast food such as Red Robin to sit in restaurants like Kabuki. Along with chances to watch either free or paid entertainment, Irvine Spectrum is a place one can go for any occasion.

Tips and Tricks

  • For free parking, go after 6PM on Monday to Friday or on the weekend.
  • UCI’s S line that runs on Saturdays goes from UCI to Irvine Spectrum from 10:30AM to 11PM for $2. There are stops at University Town Center, Puerta del Sol, Camino del Sol, and Vista del Campo Norte.
  • There is an ice rink open every year from November to early January.
  • Other attractions include the Giant Wheel, a carousel, a splash pad fountain, a kiddie train, and the clock court.
  • There is live music on Saturdays from 6PM to 9PM and Sundays from 2PM to 5PM.

Directions from UCI

Driving

  • Take University Drive and I-405 to Enterprise Drive/Entertainment Way.
  • Take a right on Enterprise Drive/Entertainment Way.
  • Turn left onto Spectrum Center Drive.

Public Transportation

  • Take University Drive and I-405 to Enterprise Drive/Entertainment Way.
  • Take a right on Enterprise Drive/Entertainment Way.
  • Turn left onto Spectrum Center Drive.
  • Head to the Campus-University Center bus stop (in front of campus).
  • Take the Orange County Transportation Authority 79 LARWIN SQUARE and get off at Culver-Irvine Center.
  • Walk on Culver Drive towards Irvine Center Drive, and turn left onto Irvine Center Drive.
  • The Irvine Center-Culver stop will be on the right.
  • Take the Orange County Transportation Authority 90 DANA POINT HARBOR until you’ve reached the Irvine Center-Spectrum stop.
  • Walk until you reach Irvine Spectrum.

Uber, Lyft and Taxi are also available options.
